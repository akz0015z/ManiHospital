About ManiHospital

ManiHospital is a modern cloud-based hospital management system designed to simplify and improve the way healthcare teams track and manage patient information. The platform focuses on making critical tasks such as reviewing conditions, assigning doctors, updating patient records, and monitoring admissions fast, efficient, and stress-free.

The system prioritizes a clean, intuitive interface that ensures staff can access real time patient details with ease. With secure backend handling and smooth visual data representation, ManiHospital enhances workflow efficiency while supporting a more organized hospital environment.
